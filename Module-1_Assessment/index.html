About Me

Hello! My name is Tom Busch, I am a recent graduate in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Computer Science from the University of Minnesota. While in college my favorite class was Introduction to Data Mining as I really enjoyed the ways data could be manipulated to find useful information and create effective predictive models.

In my free time I like to explore Minneapolis by biking on its many trails, camp in northern Minnesota, and fish the nearby lakes. I also enjoy going to theater productions at all the small and large theaters around the cities.

I hope to learn the tools to be successful as a professional developer from the Dev10 program. I have a solid grasp on computer science theory, but I do not have experience with the details on how to create an efficient program for a business. I have heard a lot of good things about the bootcamp and the program as a whole and I am looking forward to learning more about Gen10.
